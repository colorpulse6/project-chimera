## Quick Start

1. You wake up in Havenwood village. Talk to the villagers to get your bearings.
2. Find **Elder Morris** near the village center &mdash; he'll point you in the right direction.
3. Visit **Aldric's Shop** to stock up on supplies before heading out.
4. Explore the **Outskirts** &mdash; be ready for random encounters.
5. When you're strong enough, take on the **Bandit Camp**.
